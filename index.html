About

Romashko Tattoo & Piercing Studio is a space built around creativity, individuality, and self-expression. What started as a personal passion for tattoo culture grew into a place where people can feel comfortable turning their ideas into something permanent and meaningful.

Our studio is inspired by underground skate culture and the environments we grew up in. It's not just about getting a tattoo — it's about the experience, the atmosphere, and the connection between the artist and the client. From custom pieces to quick flash designs, every work is approached with attention and intention.

We focus on creating a relaxed and creative space where people can come in, hang out, and be part of something bigger than just a service. Whether it's your first tattoo or another addition to your collection, Romashko is about making the process feel real, personal, and authentic.

STYLES

We work across a range of styles, from classic blackwork to detailed color tattoos and more experimental designs. Each piece is approached individually, allowing the style to match the idea, the placement, and the person. Whether you're looking for something clean and minimal or more expressive and creative, we adapt to make it feel right.

BLACKWORK

Clean, bold designs focused on strong lines and contrast. Classic tattoo style that stays sharp and timeless.

COLOR

Bright and expressive tattoos that bring designs to life. Perfect for detailed work and creative pieces.

EXPERIMENTAL

Creative and unique designs inspired by skate culture and modern art. Less traditional, more personal and expressive.

Flash Tattoos

flash poster

flash poster mobile

Flash tattoos are ready-made designs created by our artists and available for walk-in sessions every friday! They are perfect for quick tattoos without a long consultation, while still keeping the same level of quality and attention to detail. At Romashko, flash tattoos are offered at a fixed price of $70, making them accessible, spontaneous, and part of the overall studio experience.
