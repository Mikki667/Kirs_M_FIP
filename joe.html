Joe O'Connor

Joe O'Connor is a tattoo artist from in London, Ontario. He got into tattooing through his interest in art and color, spending a lot of time experimenting and trying different things until it slowly became something serious. At first, it wasn't a clear path, but over time he found himself fully involved in the tattoo industry. With 4 years of experience, he focuses on color tattoos, working on pieces that feel alive and stand out. He believes that every person comes with their own idea and story, and his goal is to understand that and turn it into something real. Because of that, he takes time with each client, helping them choose and build a design that actually feels right for them.
