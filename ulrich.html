Ulrich Stoffer

Ulrich Stoffer is a tattoo artist and musician based in London, Ontario. He was born in London but spent a big part of his life living in Mexico, where he was surrounded by a strong culture of music and art. That environment shaped the way he sees creativity and pushed him to express himself in different ways. He started with music, and over time that same feeling translated into tattooing. With 5 years of experience, he now focuses on blackwork tattoos, creating bold and clean designs that carry a strong visual presence. For him, both music and tattooing are about expression and feeling. He pays attention to the person behind the idea, making sure every piece has meaning and fits naturally. His goal is to create tattoos that feel personal, honest, and something people can connect with over time.
